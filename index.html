Subaru WRX STI 2.5L

The Subaru STI 2.5L EJ25 engine was offered in the United States starting in 2004 thru today. The flat turbo engine is a popular engine among Subaru enthusiasts. Chevrolet 6.2L LS3

The Chevrolet 6.2L LS3 V8 engine was available in the 2009-2015 Camaro Super Sport, and the 2008-2013 Corvette. In the Camaro SS, the LS3 put out 426 horse power at 5900 RPM, with 420 lb-ft of torque t 4600 RPM. In the Corvette, the LS3 had a horse power rating between 430-436 at 5900 RPM, and a torque rating between 424-428 lb-ft, depending on the exhasut type.
